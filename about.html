About Us

Yujir Planet Competencies Pvt Ltd is a climate-focused startup dedicated to helping organizations and communities achieve net zero through building true competencies. We go beyond skills to develop integrated knowledge, attitudes, and behaviors needed to tackle complex climate challenges. By combining participatory methods, innovation, and strategic insight, we create competency ecosystems that drive lasting climate action and resilience.

Our Mission

At Yujir Planet Competencies, we build the skills and leadership needed for real-world climate action. Through coaching, consulting, and custom programs, we equip people and organizations to turn sustainability goals into impactful change. Our focus: climate education, women’s leadership, organizational growth, and talent transformation — all driving systemic, planet-positive progress.

From Awareness to Action

In exploring deeper sustainability concepts—like balancing people, planet, and profit and building a circular economy—our founder saw the missing link: Competencies. Not just knowledge, but the skills, tools, and mindsets needed to recognize climate problems and solve them through our work and everyday decisions. But even competencies alone aren’t enough.

The Yujir Difference

At Yujir Planet Competencies, we don't just deliver training—we build climate competencies. We go beyond surface-level awareness to design capacity-building frameworks that integrate climate science, systems thinking, and real-world action.

Our work bridges the gap between intention and impact through participatory learning, strategic consulting, and operational tools that drive meaningful climate action. Whether it's through digital emissions education, leadership development, or sustainability interventions, we equip individuals and organizations with the ability to think, adapt, and lead under uncertainty.

This focus on transformation—not just information—is what sets us apart. That’s the Yujir difference.
