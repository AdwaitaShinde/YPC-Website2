Building Capacity for a Climate-Resilient Future

At Yujir Planet Competencies, we design and deliver strategic, action-oriented services that equip individuals, communities, and organizations to lead in the face of climate challenges. Our offerings foster deep climate intelligence, operational resilience, and purpose-driven leadership through tailored interventions that drive measurable impact.

Our Services

Climate Awareness & Training

We deliver interactive workshops, webinars, and experiential tools like card games to raise awareness on climate literacy, digital emissions, e-waste, and sustainable practices. Our programs cater to schools, corporates, and grassroots communities, fostering inclusive and actionable climate understanding.

Climate Consulting & Strategy

We support MSMEs, enterprises, and institutions in designing tailored sustainability strategies, including low-carbon interventions, CSR-aligned climate projects, and ecosystem-based frameworks. Our consulting empowers clients to take climate-positive action rooted in measurable outcomes and accountability.

Social Impact & Leadership Development

We cultivate climate-conscious leaders through coaching, mentorship, and capacity-building programs focused on systems thinking, adaptive leadership, and inclusive participation. These initiatives enable individuals and organizations to lead change, foster resilience, and drive social transformation.
