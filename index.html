Our Journey

Yujir Planet Competencies is a climate-focused startup empowering organizations and communities to move toward net zero. We design actionable frameworks that build true climate competencies—integrating skills, mindsets, and behaviors needed to tackle today’s climate challenges. By combining innovation, participation, and systems thinking, we foster climate intelligence and resilience. We believe real change needs more than training—it needs ecosystems that sustain climate action.

More About Us

Our Vision

To build a world where every organization and community is equipped with the competencies, systems, and mindset needed to lead lasting, meaningful climate action—shaping a resilient and regenerative future for all.
